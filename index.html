Phase 0: Mental Model Reset

  • ‚úì Explain why a qubit is not a faster bit (without using the word "superposition" as magic)
  • ‚úì Describe why measurement destroys quantum information (and why this matters for QA/debugging)
  • ‚úì Understand that quantum probability is fundamentally different from classical randomness
  • ‚úì Recognize when someone is misusing quantum terms (e.g., "quantum AI" marketing BS)
Self-Test:

Can you explain to a friend why quantum computers aren't just "faster at searching"? If yes, you're ready for Phase 1.

Phase 1: Qubits & Gates

You've completed Phase 1 when you can:

  • ‚úì Visualize any single-qubit state on the Bloch sphere (given angles Œ∏ and œÜ)

You've completed Phase 2 when you can:

  • ‚úì Implement Deutsch-Jozsa for n=3 qubits with a custom oracle

  • ‚úì Describe why Shor's algorithm threatens RSA (period finding ‚Üí factoring)
  • ‚úì Know when to use classical vs quantum approaches (e.g., why sorted data doesn't need Grover's)
